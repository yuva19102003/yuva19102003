## What I Do

I design, automate, and operate **production-grade cloud infrastructure** across AWS, GCP, Azure, and DigitalOcean — with a strong focus on:

- Infrastructure as Code with Terraform and Ansible
- CI/CD pipelines that ship reliably across dev, staging, and production
- Security built in from the start, not bolted on after
- Observability that explains failures, not just shows graphs

I turn complex, fragile systems into **boring, predictable platforms** that engineering teams can trust and operators can sleep on.
